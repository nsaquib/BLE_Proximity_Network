PrNet

PrNet is a network of sensors that measure proximity in the Montessori classroom.

About

A crucial part of Montessori education is observation of the students, so teachers can assist individuals as needed. Our work aims to assist this observation by measuring proximity of students through Simblee COM sensors. We provide detailed visualizations in a dashboard-style interface to both teachers and parents. This dashboard helps teachers individualize their own methods to facilitate a childâ€™s growth in the classroom.


This project is part of the Wildflower School network.
